## Full description

Plannio is a free date-poll tool with built-in Discord and Slack channel updates.

Create a poll with specific dates or multi-day ranges in a calendar or list view. Share one link with your group — in Discord, Slack, email, or anywhere else. Everyone marks which dates work for them; results update live on the poll page.

**What makes Plannio different**

- **Discord & Slack channel updates** — Connect a channel when you create a poll. Plannio posts when the poll is created, when someone votes, when a date is locked, and when reminders go out. Uses Discord/Slack webhooks — **no bot invite** to your server.
- **Expected responses** — Set how many people should answer (e.g. 8/8). See progress on the poll and get a channel message when everyone has voted.
- **Vote without an account** — Voters can respond with just a name; only the poll creator needs to sign in.
- **Reminders** — Schedule automatic nudges or send a manual "Send reminder now" from the poll page.
- **Date ranges** — Propose full weekends or multi-day options in one poll.

**Good for**

Game nights and community events in Discord, team meeting times in Slack, weekend trips with friends, remote team scheduling, and any group that is tired of chasing "works for me" replies in chat.

**Pricing**

Free — no paywall on features that are live today.

**Sign-in**

Poll creators: Discord, Slack, or email/password. Voters: optional login, or name-only.
